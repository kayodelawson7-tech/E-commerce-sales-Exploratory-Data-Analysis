# E-commerce-sales-Exploratory-Data-Analysis

## Project Overview
This internship project demonstrates the complete data analytics workflow using an e-commerce sales dataset. It includes data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualization to reveal sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and key business insights.

## Problem Statement:
E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional data daily. However, without proper analysis, it is difficult to identify sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, preferred payment methods, and the products that contribute most to revenue. this limits the organization's ability to make informed business decisions and optimize sales performance. Therefore, there is a need to analyze the available sales data to uncover patterns and provide insights that support strategic decision-making.

## Objectives
To analyze the sales performance of the e-commerce business.
To identify the best-selling products.
To determine the payment methods most preferred by customers.
To evaluate the effectiveness of different referral sources.
To examine trends in order status and customer purchasing behavior.
To provide data-driven recommendations for improving sales performance.
4. Dataset Description
This project is based on a transactional e-commerce sales dataset stored in CSV format. The dataset contains 1,200 customer order transactions across 14 variables, including OrderID, Date, Product, Quantity, UnitPrice, TotalPrice, PaymentMethod, OrderStatus, ReferralSource, and CouponCode. These variables provide the foundation for data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, and the extraction of meaningful business insights.

## Tools used
Python Programming language used for data cleaning and analysis.
Pandas (a library) Data cleaning and manipulation.
NumPy (a library) Numerical computations
Matplotlib Data visualization.

## Observation 
The analysis shows that most customers place low-value orders, resulting in a positively skewed distribution of total sales. Monthly sales revenue fluctuated considerably between 2023 and 2025, with no clear upward or downward trend, although sales improved toward the end of the period. Instagram generated the highest sales revenue among referral sources, while Referral contributed the least. Chair and Printer were the top-selling products based on total sales revenue, closely followed by Laptop, whereas Phone generated the lowest sales revenue. Online payment was the most frequently used payment method, although customers generally used a wide variety of payment options. Order statuses were relatively evenly distributed, but the high number of cancelled and returned orders suggests potential issues with customer satisfaction or the ordering process. Customer purchasing behaviour also indicates low customer retention, as most customers made only one purchase and no individual customer stood out in terms of order frequency, highlighting limited repeat purchasing.

## Recommendation
The business should focus on increasing the average order value by encouraging customers to purchase more through product bundles, cross-selling, and minimum-spend incentives. To achieve more consistent sales, it should analyse the factors behind high-performing months and apply successful marketing and promotional strategies during slower periods. Since Instagram is the strongest referral source, the business should continue investing in it while improving the performance of weaker channels such as Referral through incentive programmes. The high number of cancelled and returned orders should be investigated by analysing customer feedback and improving product quality, descriptions, order fulfilment, and customer service. Finally, the business should strengthen customer retention by implementing loyalty programmes, personalised offers, targeted email campaigns, and other incentives to encourage repeat purchases and build long-term customer loyalty.
